Wednesday.
//Wednesday September 9, 1998 -GHS
Even Newton would end up laughing at how I am going to school in obedience to the Law of Inertia.
I walk the hills.
The inclines are severe, as always.
Pace grows slower.
At last I come out into a flat place.

[Taichi]
"...!"

Broiled by a premonition, awareness heats up.
The body moves faster than rationality.
I turned around!

[Taichi]
"......not there?"

But I felt the presence of a person.
